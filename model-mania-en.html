Model Mania is a design challenge that has been taking place since 2000 in which modeling skills are measured, both in precision and time, for engineers and designers who want to compete and show their SolidWorks skills during the annual Dassault Systemes’s conference.

In 2008, the structural analysis of the piece was added to determine its safety factor and increase the difficulty of the event.

These are all the designs up to date made by me.
