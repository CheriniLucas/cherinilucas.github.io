About me

I am Lucas Cherini, a Master Builder and an advanced Mechanical Engineering student.

Previously, I worked in construction as a Master Builder, where I provided consulting, project management, supervision, and construction of residential works, as well as electrical, plumbing, and gas installations.

Additionally, I worked in the manufacturing industry as a Methods and Time Analyst. There, I was responsible for the development, control, and optimization of the manufacturing processes for the Alcatel ONE TOUCH production lines, and I also assisted with the Motorola, Noblex, Lenovo, and Sony lines.

During my studies, I worked at the Aerospace Technology Center in the piping area, where, alongside VENG and CONAE, we developed the new rocket engine test stand for the Tronador II project.

Currently, I am working at Solaer Ingenieria as a Computational Structural Engineer, where I designed and validated advanced FEM models for mechanical structures, optimizing performance and reducing material costs. I also conducted detailed structural analysis in compliance with ASME, API, CIRSOC standards, ensuring regulatory approval and operational safety, and I created engineering reports and technical documentation for design validation and project certification.

I have extensive experience in design and simulation with Abaqus, Catia, SolidWorks, AutoCAD, MatLab, C, Python, and Arduino.
