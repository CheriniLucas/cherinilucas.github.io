About me

I am Lucas Cherini, a native of the city of Ushuaia. Since I was very young, I have been passionate about design and mechanics, which led me to study at a technical school where I started with a degree in electromechanics and later in construction. At the age of 18 I graduated as a "Maestro Mayor de Obras" (similar to a junior architectural degree) and got my first formal job in an architecture studio as a project draftsman. There, my main tasks were to make the architectural drawings, do the presentations and follow up the municipal files, and to compute and budget the constrution work. After a year and a half I decided to look for new challenges and began to work independently, while dabbling in automotive mechanics.

That same year I was offered to join a manufacturing company as an operator, where I carried out tasks of assembly and control of the operation of air conditioners, video cameras and notebooks. In turn, on weekends, I participated in industrial maintenance. After a year and a half I was promoted to Methods & Times Analyst where I was in charge of the production, control and optimization of the manufacturing processes of the lines of Alcatel cell phones and Noblex tablets; and as an auxiliary for the lines of Motorola mobile phones, Sanyo cameras and Lenovo notebooks.

It was at this stage where my desire to study a professional career reappeared, so I saved everything I could, sold all my things, finished the pending buildings and in 2015 we came to study with my life partner at the National University of La Plata.

The first years I devoted myself entirely to studies, but for economic reasons, in 2018 I resumed some freelance as a draftsman, and now I am looking for the opportunity to dedicate myself to my area of study. My main interest is in mechanical design and numerical analysis, along with industrial maintenance tasks.

From my technical degree and years of work I obtained a strong preparation in the use of AutoCAD and Microsoft Office, and with the years of Engineering in La Plata I added SolidWorks, MatLab, C and Python to my repertoire.

I am a responsible, curious and restless person. I constantly seek perfection in the most efficient way.
