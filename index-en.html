About me

I'm Lucas Cherini, I started studying Mechanical Engineering a little late (at 24 years old) because I was financially limited, but with the advantage of having experienced the world of work in construction and the manufacturing industry. This helped me to find an immediate use for some things I was learning, and to question some others.

Throughout my years as a student, a multitude of concerns have arisen about topics that were not addressed or deepened at the level that I wanted in college, which led me to develop small investigations, analyzes and projects on my own.

Some of them are available on this page for those who feel the same insatiable curiosity, and I hope it motivate you to continue developing the topics that you are most passionate about.
