High School!

In high school, after switching to a technical one, I started using AutoCAD on a daily basis, where reading the manual on my own gave me a fundamental advantage that helped me better understand and use the new knowledge I acquired.

I did the first three years specializing in electromechanics, but then I opted for construction where I did another three years until I graduated. At the end I decided to continue an extra year to obtain the title of "Maestro Mayor de Obras" (similar to a junior architectural degree), where we delve into all the knowledge acquired and apply it in real cases.
