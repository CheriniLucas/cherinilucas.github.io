LEGO Technic!

LEGO was a significant part of my childhood that I remember fondly. Assembling and disassembling everything around me helped me develop my imagination and ingenuity to create new worlds, and more importantly, it brought me closer to the world of engineering.

The Technic collection is undoubtedly my favorite, and this particular model was the first one I received when I was 8 years old. I can’t imagine a better one to start this section with.

Model Mania!

Model Mania is a design challenge that has been held since 2000, measuring the modeling skills of engineers and designers who wish to compete and showcase their abilities in SolidWorks during the annual Dassault Systemes conference.

In 2008, structural analysis of the part was added to determine its safety factor and increase the difficulty of the event.
